About Kenya Petitions

Kenya Petitions is a platform empowering citizens to drive change in public sectors like health, education, and governance through signature collection.

Our mission is to create a transparent space for Kenyans to raise issues, support causes, and engage authorities, fostering accountability and democracy.

Rooted in the Kenyan Constitution (Articles 37, 119, 257), we champion the right to petition. Join us to make your voice heard.
