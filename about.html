About Kenya Petitions

Empowering citizens to drive meaningful change through collective action

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To create a transparent digital platform where every Kenyan can raise issues, support causes, and engage with authorities to foster accountability and participatory democracy. We bridge the gap between citizens and decision-makers through technology.

Our Vision

A Kenya where every citizen's voice matters in shaping public policy and governance. We envision a future where petitions lead to tangible solutions for community challenges, creating a more responsive and accountable government.

Our Core Values

Integrity

We maintain the highest standards of transparency in petition verification and signature validation. Every action on our platform is accountable and traceable.

Inclusivity

Our platform serves all Kenyans regardless of location, background, or status. We prioritize accessibility for marginalized communities.

Innovation

We continuously improve our technology to enhance user experience while maintaining robust security and data protection standards.

Constitutional Foundation

Legal Basis

Kenya Petitions operates under the following constitutional provisions:

Kenyan Constitution

Article 37

Guarantees every person the right to peaceably and unarmed assemble, demonstrate, picket, and present petitions to public authorities.

Article 119

Provides for the right to petition Parliament to consider any matter within its authority, including petitioning to enact, amend or repeal any legislation.

Article 257

Establishes provisions for popular initiatives through collection of signatures from registered voters.
