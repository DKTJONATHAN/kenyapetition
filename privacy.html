Privacy Policy

Kenya Petitions is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Data Collection

We collect your full name, county, declaration, and signature initials when you sign a petition, used only for petition submission.

Data Use

Your data is used to: - Record petition support. - Submit to authorities. - Provide updates (if opted-in).

Data Security

We use standard security measures, but advise caution as no platform is fully secure. However, we delete all data as soon as petitions are submitted.

Your Rights

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your data. Contact us at: jonathanmwaniki07@gmail.com .
