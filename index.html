How Citizen Petitions Shape Kenya

1 The Constitutional Right to Petition

Kenya's Article 37 guarantees your right to petition, while Article 257 allows constitutional amendments through 1 million verified signatures - a power exercised during the 2020 BBI process.

Did you know? The 2010 Constitution was the first in Kenyan history to explicitly protect petition rights.

2 Real Impact Through Signatures

Recent successful petitions prove change is possible:

  • 2024 Finance Bill: 1.8M signatures led to President Ruto withdrawing controversial tax proposals
  • 2023 Medical Interns: 320,000 signatures forced the Health Ministry to release delayed internship funds
  • 2022 NHIF Reforms: 640K signatures contributed to corruption investigations

Petition success rate has increased from 22% (2013-2017) to 41% (2018-2024) as institutions become more responsive.
